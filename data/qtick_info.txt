QTick Platform Features

1. Smart Queuing & Appointments
The core of QTick. Customers join remotely and track their status via the app, eliminating physical lines and minimizing uncertainty.
- Reduce physical waiting times significantly.
- Provide real-time status updates to phones.
- Build trust by respecting customer time.

2. Billing & Inventory
Simplify financial transactions. Generate professional digital invoices instantly and track stock levels automatically to manage costs effectively.
- Create seamless end-to-end customer journeys.
- Auto-deduct products from inventory upon sale.
- Keep supplies well-stocked with tracking.

3. Automated Review System
Build critical social proof. The system prompts users for feedback immediately after service, automating the collection of testimonials.
- Showcase positive reviews on your public profile.
- Build trust and credibility for new research.
- Automate the entire feedback loop.

4. Loyalty Program
Foster customer retention, which is more cost-effective than acquisition. Identify repeat customers and create targeted reward programs.
- Turn satisfied clients into loyal advocates.
- Encourage repeat business with special offers.
- Automated tracking of customer points.

5. Auto-Generated Website
Every business needs an online presence. QTick generates an SEO-optimized profile that acts as a mini-website for your brand.
- Showcase services, pricing, and operating hours.
- Get discovered by new customers on Google.
- Display glowing customer reviews in one place.

6. WhatsApp Digital Campaigns
Turn your database into a marketing asset. Run targeted campaigns via WhatsApp to re-engage past clients and attract new ones.
- Reach specific segments with special offers.
- Boost sales during slower periods instantly.
- Drive immediate bookings with high open rates.

7. Advanced Data Analytics
Put data at the forefront of your strategy. Gather insights on footfall, service times, and revenue to make informed decisions.
- Identify peak hours and popular services.
- Pinpoint areas for improvement.
- Shape your business for future growth.

8. Customer Lead Generation & Retention
Provide an exceptional experience to turn customers into brand ambassadors who generate referrals.
- Make your business highly visible online.
- Attract a steady stream of new customers.
- Drive organic growth via your profile.

9. Comprehensive Reports
Generate detailed weekly, monthly, and yearly reports on key metrics like revenue, acquisition, and staff performance.
- Invaluable for financial planning.
- Review staff performance effectively.
- Make long-term strategic decisions.

10. Central Enquiry Hub
A single place to capture and track new enquiries. Easily review incoming leads to increase your client base.
- Never miss a business opportunity.
- Centralized dashboard for all leads.
- Boost conversion from enquiry to client.
